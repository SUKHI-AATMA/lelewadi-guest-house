About Us

Lele Wadi Guest House is situated in the costal village of Varsoli It is about 1 km. from Alibag bus stand and 3 minutes walk from Varsoli Sea Beach.

The term ‘Wadi’ in local term is tree plantation / garden. Lele Wadi Guest House is among a garden of Coconut, Beetle nut and other trees.

It is approved by MTDC i.e. Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation. Lele Wadi Guest House has total 5 double bed rooms with attached toilets & with other modern amenities.

Things to do

Beaches

Varsoli beach is at a walking distance from the property. Other beaches like Khim, Alibaug, Nagaon, Kashid etc are at a driving distance as well. Water sports can be done at Mandwa beach, Nagaon beach and Alibaug beach.

Forts

Kolaba fort & Hirakot Old fort is the nearest forts to the property. There are other forts like Murud, Khanderi, Underi, Revdanda, Korlai.

Temples

Shree Kshetra Kanakeshwar on the Konkan coast near Alibag, is a popular place, famous for its cool climate and the old temple of Shiva. other temples like Shri Datt Temple and Birla Temple are one of the few worth a visit.
